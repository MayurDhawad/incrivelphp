Vision & Mission

Vision

"To provide a supportive and transformative learning environment to help students reach their potential and successfully achieve their career aspirations".

Mission

To offer a diverse array of innovative proprietary academic programs both on-campus and off-campus to foster academic excellence and competitive spirit through the constant engagement of students and faculty members in a dynamic and congenial learning environment. Through excellence and innovations in teaching and learning, SPARK will be recognized by the students, academic community, and the general public as a professional and premier institution of quality, dedication and distinction that engages all the students for producing resulting careers.
